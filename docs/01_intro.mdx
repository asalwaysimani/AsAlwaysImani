A Queer Black Woman who has been doing all the things all over the internet. Having existed on the internet in form or another since the late 90s, As Always Imani is the evolution of the long online presence of its creator Imani's on the internet. Imani can be found writing multidisciplinary creative works, designing and developing narrative focused analog and digital games, producing independent documentary projects, and streaming on Twitch. 

They are in a long term requited emotional relationship with Pop Punk. Their hobbies include recommending people new to them music and collecting new hobbies. Your favorite local Ace cryptid and a constantly screaming ball of rage, anxiety, and depression.
